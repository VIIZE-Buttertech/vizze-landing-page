Frequently Asked Questions

Find the answers to your questions about our pricing, cameras, parking, and available regions. If you need more detailed information, feel free to contact us.

How is the pricing structured per camera?

Our pricing is based on a per-camera model. For example, the Free plan covers up to 3 cameras, while the Business plan supports up to 10 cameras, and the Enterprise plan offers unlimited cameras. Additional cameras can be added based on your needs.

Are parking spots included in the pricing plans?

Yes, parking spots are included in certain plans. The Free plan includes 1 parking spot, while the Business plan provides 5 spots, and the Enterprise plan offers unlimited spots. You can scale based on your requirements.

Which regions are supported for these plans?

Our services are available globally, but availability varies by region. The Free plan is limited to select regions, while the Business and Enterprise plans have broader coverage. You can check the supported regions on our Region Availability Page.

Can I customize the plans for larger setups?

Yes! Our Enterprise plan is designed for larger-scale operations with custom needs. You can reach out to our sales team to discuss specific requirements, including camera, parking spot, and region customizations.
